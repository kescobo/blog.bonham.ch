Sesshin of the Original Face

July 20, 2019
poem•meditation•sesshin•zen•personal•bowz

The 2019 summer sesshin of Boundless Way Zen just wrapped up yesterday. I had the honor to serve as Jiki (time keeper).

The water must know
If it is hot
or it is cold.
Is this true?
As to Mu,
Though I've been told,
I know not.
Does it show?
